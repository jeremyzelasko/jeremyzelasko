Overview

This lesson is a over Margins and Padding. We will start with a quick review over the Box Model as both margin and padding deal directly with the box model. Then I will talk about Margins followed by padding and then how to edit either of them.

Editing

Editing principles of Margin and Padding are exaclty the same with exception of the prefix you use.

Both can be edited either holistically, meaning all sides at once, or just editing a certain side.

To do so choose which you are editing (margin or padding) for this example we will use margin. Then simple state you are editing that and which side joined with a "-"

For Example:

  • Margin:
  • Margin-top
  • Margin-left:
  • Marin-right:
  • Margin-bottom:

Pretty simple. The first option is kind of special. If you put just one number it will edit all sides however it you put mulitple numbers it changes.

Exp: Margin: 25px 50px 30 px 70 px; This would change the margin as follows:

  • Top – 25px
  • Right – 50px
  • Bottom – 30px
  • Left – 70px

If the margin has three numbers - Margin: 5px 10px 15px; The margin would change as follows:

  • Top - 5px
  • Right and left – 10 px
  • Bottom – 25px

If it has two numbers - Margin: 5px 10px;

  • Top and bottom - 5px
  • Right and Left - 10px

Again, all principles are the same for padding, simple replace the word "margin" with "padding".
